Cleaning Project

The origins of cleaning can be traced back to 2800 BC. Archaeologists discovered soap-like materials and various cylinders, indicating that ancient Babylonians began making soap around this time. Inscriptions on these cylinders read, "fat boiled with ash." Cleaning was also practiced by other ancient civilizations, including the Egyptians. A medical document from 1500 BC, for example, shows that these people used vegetable and animal oil to make soap-like substances. They also used alkaline soap to wash.

Weather App

The state of the atmosphere, including temperature, atmospheric pressure, wind, humidity, precipitation, and cloud cover, is referred to as weather. It differs from climate, which is the average of all weather conditions for a specific location over about 30 years. This Weather app collects data from various locations around the world. The primary goal of this weather app is to accurately display the weather minutely, hourly, and daily, as well as a forecast for 5 days with all precise temperature degrees measurements in celcius and fahrenheit.
